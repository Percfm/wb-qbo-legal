Privacy Policy

Work Order → QuickBooks application · Last updated: July 18, 2026

1. Who we are

This application ("the App") is a private, internal tool operated by 228850 Ontario Ltd, operating as Perth County Fabrication & Machining ("we", "us"), of 4812 Rd 164, Bornholm, ON N0K 1A0, Canada. It is used only by our own staff to process our own business records. It is not offered to the public and does not create accounts for third parties.

2. What the App does

The App digitizes our handwritten shop work orders and prepares draft sales invoices in our own QuickBooks Online company. A photo of a work order is read to extract the customer, project, and line items, which a staff member reviews and then posts to QuickBooks.

3. Information accessed through Intuit / QuickBooks

With authorization to our own QuickBooks Online company, the App accesses:

  • Reads: company information, customers and projects, and products/services (items) — used to match work-order lines to the correct project and inventory item.
  • Writes (only when a staff member confirms): creates a draft invoice and attaches the source work-order photo to it. Invoices are created unsent and reviewed in QuickBooks before delivery.

The App does not access QuickBooks data belonging to anyone other than our own company.

4. How information is stored and shared

  • Work-order photos, extracted data, and QuickBooks OAuth tokens are stored on our own self-hosted server. They are not stored on any third-party cloud platform except as described below.
  • To read handwriting from the photo, the image is sent to Anthropic (Claude API) for text extraction. Anthropic processes it to return the extracted text and does not use it to train models. See Anthropic's privacy policy.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share QuickBooks data with any other third party.

5. Retention

Work-order photos and extracted records are retained on our server for as long as needed for our accounting records, then deleted per our internal retention practices.

6. Security

Access to the App and its data is restricted to authorized staff. OAuth tokens are stored locally and can be revoked at any time from within QuickBooks (Apps → this app → Disconnect).

7. Your choices

Because the App uses only our own company's data, there are no external user accounts. The QuickBooks company owner may disconnect the App at any time, which revokes its access.
