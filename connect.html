Connect QuickBooks

Work Order → QuickBooks is a private, internal tool for 228850 Ontario Ltd o/a Perth County Fabrication & Machining. It runs on the shop's own computer, not in the cloud.

To connect or reconnect

  1. Open the app on the shop computer: http://localhost:8200
  2. Click Connect QuickBooks and authorize the company.

Access can be revoked at any time from QuickBooks (Apps → this app → Disconnect) or from the app itself. See our Privacy Policy and EULA.
